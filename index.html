    At Alu World, our portfolio reflects a commitment to quality, precision, and innovation in aluminum and contracting projects. Over the years, we've partnered across sectors to deliver solutions that combine functionality with modern design. Here’s a selection showcasing our expertise in aluminum fabrication and structural contracting.

    1. Commercial Building Facades and Curtain Walls

    Our team has transformed the exteriors of numerous commercial buildings with custom-designed aluminum facades and curtain wall systems. These projects highlight our ability to enhance building aesthetics while ensuring durability and energy efficiency.

    2. Residential Windows, Doors, and Skylights

    From sleek, modern sliding doors to high-quality aluminum-framed windows, our residential projects prioritize style and comfort. We specialize in custom designs that complement architectural elements and enhance the natural lighting of any space.

    3. Office Partitions and Glass Walls

    We’ve created dynamic and adaptable workspaces with custom aluminum and glass partitions. These installations are not only functional but add a touch of sophistication and openness to office interiors.

    4. Aluminum Cladding and Paneling

    Our aluminum cladding projects include durable, weather-resistant exterior panels for both commercial and residential clients. These projects demonstrate our expertise in providing both aesthetic and protective solutions to enhance building facades.

    5. Railings, Balconies, and Outdoor Structures

    Our portfolio includes numerous outdoor aluminum structures, from safety-compliant railings to custom balcony installations. These projects highlight our focus on providing secure, visually appealing outdoor elements.

    6. Renovations and Retrofitting Projects

    Our team has successfully completed several renovation projects, updating and enhancing existing structures with modern aluminum solutions. These projects allow us to showcase our flexibility in adapting to and enhancing established environments.


    Each project in our portfolio reflects our dedication to meeting and exceeding client expectations through quality craftsmanship, meticulous planning, and a client-focused approach. At Alu World, we are committed to building with excellence.
